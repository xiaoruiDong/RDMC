How to cite RDMC
================

Text form

.. code-block:: tex

    Dong, X., Pattanaik, L., Li, S.-C., Spiekermann, K., Pang, H.-W., Zheng, J.W., Green, William H (2025). RDMC: Reaction Data and Molecular Conformer (Version 1.0.0) [Computer software]. https://github.com/xiaoruiDong/RDMC

.. code-block:: tex

    @misc{RDMC,
        author = {Dong, Xiaorui and Pattanaik, Lagnajit and Li, Shih-Cheng and Spiekermann, Kevin and Pang, Hao-Wei and Zheng, Jonathan W. and Green, William H.},

        year = {2025},
        publisher = {GitHub},
        howpublished = {\url{https://github.com/xiaoruiDong/RDMC}}}
